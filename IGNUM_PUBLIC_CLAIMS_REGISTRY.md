## 13. 5-Question Approval Checklist

Before any piece goes out:
1. Does it use the exact master descriptor?
2. Does every claim exist in this registry?
3. Is the audience correct for the claim state?
4. Are conditional claims being used as if they were public?
5. Is there any grandiloquent inflation or category drift?

**If any answer is NO → the piece does not go out.**
